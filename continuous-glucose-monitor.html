How to apply and use our EverChek CGM?

Three simple steps to get started —apply, pair, and monitor glucose data in real time on your smartphone.

Step 1: Apply

After cleaning your skin, remove the lower cover of the EverChek CGM and place the applicator firmly and vertically on your arm. When you hear a click, the sensor has been successfully applied.

Step 2: Pair & warm-up

Download the EverChek app, scan the QR code, and complete Bluetooth pairing. Wait 1 hour for the sensor warm-up to complete before readings begin.

Step 3: Monitor

View real-time glucose data on your phone and receive high/low alerts. Log daily activities such as diet and exercise and gain clearer insights into your glucose patterns. Remove the transmitter after 14 days.
