EverChek patient app features

A complete set of patient-focused glucose management features, ready for your brand.

Real-time glucose monitoring

Stay informed with real-time glucose updates, automatically synced from your CGM sensor to your phone and smartwatch.

Current reading, trend arrow, and 24-hour glucose chart are displayed clearly on one screen, making it easy to track glucose changes at a glance.

Glucose trend visualization & prediction

View glucose trends over 3, 6, 12, and 24 hours with an intuitive line chart. Directional arrows show where your glucose is heading, helping you make informed decisions about meals, exercise, and insulin.

High/low glucose alerts

Set personalized high and low glucose thresholds and receive timely alerts when your glucose approaches or exceeds these limits, helping you take proactive action.

Event logging

Log meals, exercise, insulin doses, and medication with just a few taps. Each event is timestamped and overlaid on your glucose trend chart, helping you connect daily activities with glucose changes.

History records & statistics

Review daily, weekly, and monthly glucose summaries with key metrics including time-in-range, average glucose, and estimated HbA1c. Generate detailed reports to share with your healthcare provider.
